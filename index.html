Part 2: Reading Comprehension

Read the email about a business trip. Then, answer True (T) or False (F). Correct the false statements.

To: Team

Subject: My business trip to Bogota

Hi everyone,

I returned from Bogota last night. The trip was excellent, but very exhausting. I arrived on Wednesday morning and went directly to the hotel. The conference started at 2:00 PM. I didn't have time to eat lunch! On Thursday, I presented our new project. The managers were very happy with the results. Fortunately, it wasn't all work. On Friday evening, I visited Monserrate and ate delicious local food. I took a lot of photos. See you in the office tomorrow!

Best,
Carlos

1. Carlos arrived in Bogota on Wednesday night.

2. He ate a big lunch before the conference.

3. The presentation was on Thursday.

4. Carlos didn't visit any tourist places.

5. The managers liked the new project.

Part 3: Language Usage

Controlled Production

A. The Networking Event

Formulate the missing questions based on your colleague's answers. Pay attention to DID, WAS, and WERE.

You: 1.

Colleague: I worked at Microsoft for five years.

You: 2.

Colleague: I left because I wanted to move to a different city.

You: 3.

Colleague: Yes, the salary was excellent.

You: 4.

Colleague: No, I didn't manage a team. I was an individual contributor.

B. A Coworker's Weekend

Complete the text using the correct Simple Past form of the verbs in parentheses.

Last weekend, I 1. (be) very busy. On Saturday, I 2. (wake up) early and 3. (go) to the supermarket. In the afternoon, I 4. (study) for my master's degree. My friends 5. (invite) me to a restaurant, but I 6. (not/go) because I 7. (be) tired.

C. Pronunciation Check
