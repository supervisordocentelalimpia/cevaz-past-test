Part 2: Reading Comprehension

Read the email about a weekend trip. Then, answer True (T) or False (F). Correct the false statements.

To: Team

Subject: My camping trip!

Hi everyone,

I returned from my camping trip last night. The weekend was interesting, but very exhausting. My friends and I drove to the campsite on Saturday morning. First, we put up the tent. Then we built a fire, cooked a delicious dinner, and told stories around the fire. On Sunday, we went fishing in the river. Honestly, I didn't really like camping because it was cold, but I learned a lot. The drive home on Sunday evening was terrible. It took three hours to get back! See you all tomorrow!

Best,
Lacey

1. Lacey and her friends arrived at the campsite on Sunday morning.

2. They cooked a big dinner before building the fire.

3. The camping experience was liked by Lacey from the beginning.

4. Lacey didn't do any sport or activity on Sunday.

5. The drive home was very exhausting and long.

(invite) some friends over, so they cooked a great meal. Tim 6. (not / study) at all on Sunday. Instead, he 7. (go) to the mall and shopped for clothes.
